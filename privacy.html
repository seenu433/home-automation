Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 20, 2025
Last Updated: October 20, 2025

Overview

The Srini Home Automation Alexa skill ("the Skill") is designed to provide home automation control and announcement services. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Skill.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.
  • Voice Recordings: We do not store or access your voice recordings.
  • Device Data: We do not collect information about your personal devices or usage patterns.
  • Location Data: We do not collect or store your location information.
  • User Profiles: We do not create or maintain user profiles or accounts.

How the Skill Works

The Skill processes your voice commands in real-time to:

  • Control home automation devices through Azure Functions
  • Send announcements to specified home zones (bedroom, downstairs, upstairs, all)
  • Monitor door events and water leak alerts
  • Process Custom Tasks for Alexa Routines

All processing is done server-side through Azure Functions, and no user data is retained after processing.

Data Processing

  • Temporary Processing: Voice commands are processed temporarily to execute home automation functions.
  • No Storage: No conversation data, commands, or responses are stored.
  • Secure Communication: All communications use HTTPS encryption.
  • Azure Integration: The Skill uses Azure Service Bus for device communication, with no personal data transmission.

Third-Party Services

This Skill integrates with:

  • Amazon Alexa: Subject to Amazon's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Microsoft Azure: Used for hosting and message processing, subject to Microsoft's Privacy Statement

Children's Privacy

This Skill is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
